## A11y CoP Working Session

### Summary

Meeting discussions focused on streamlining administrative scribe workflows and pivoting accessibility strategies toward business-driven leadership advocacy.

**Scribe Workflow Streamlining**  
The team decided to simplify the scribe process by prioritizing the generated notes section over editing full transcripts. This adjustment aims to increase efficiency for future session documentation.

**Accessibility Strategy Shift**  
Participants agreed to reframe accessibility as a business advantage for leadership buy-in rather than solely a moral imperative. They identified data-driven case studies as essential for proving value.

**OKRs and Scheduling**  
The group plans to propose company-wide accessibility objectives to ensure protected time and resource allocation. They also committed to rescheduling sessions to better accommodate leadership and global participants.

### Decisions

* **Scribe workflow limited to notes section** The scribe workflow is modified to focus review efforts solely on the notes section of the transcript rather than the full document to improve efficiency.

* **Accessibility OKR strategy established** The team will offer example accessibility OKRs to the company and pursue the creation of a company-wide accessibility OKR through leadership alignment.

* **CoP company-wide presentation strategy established** The Community of Practice will present to the company to formally announce the group, invite participation, offer example OKRs, and outline planned accessibility training.

* **Meeting scheduling and format update** The meeting will be moved to a later time in the day to prioritize executive attendance, with a shift to starting at quarter-past or quarter-to the hour to improve scheduling flexibility.
